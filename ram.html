Education

Ram Pothineni has been very interested in acting since his childhood, that's why there is no more information on his education is found. He does his schooling at a well-known school in Chennai, Chettinad Vidyasharam, and for his further education, he attends Chennai University. After this, he worked in the Telegu film industry.

Acting career

Ram Pothineni was fond of working in films since childhood, and because of this, he started acting in films at a very early age. When he was only 14 years old, he got a chance to work on a short film in the Tamil language, and the name of that film was Adayaalam which was released in the year 2002. In this film, he played the role of a drug addict who was 18 years old. Four years after working on a short film in the Tamil language, he got a chance to do a theatrical debuted through the Telegu film Devadasu. This film was released in the year 2006, and at that time, he was only 18 years old. Through this film, he got a chance to show his acting capabilities to the world and also won many awards due to this. This film was successful at the box office, and through this, Ram was able to win the audience's support.

Awards

  • In the year 2002, he won the Europe Film Festival, Switzerland Award in the category of Best Actor for the film Adayaalam.
  • For the film Devadasu in the year 2007, he won South Filmfare Awards in the category of Best Male Debut.
  • He is also a winner of the Zee Cine Awards Telugu in the category of Sensational Star of the Year in the year 2020.
  • He is also featured in Hyderabad Times' Most Desirable Men list many times. He featured in the year 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020.
